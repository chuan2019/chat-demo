Welcome To the Customer Service Chat System

As an Analyst, you can login with a unique nickname. After login, you can receive chatting request(s) from clients.

As a client, you can login with a unique nickname. After login, you can start chatting anytime. An analyst will be automatically picked to help answer any of your questions.

Due to time constraint, current version of the app does not have UI support yet. All chatting services are provided via APIs.
